Internet Technology

Introduction to Internet technology

Internet, also known as 'net' is a worldwide area network connecting all the computer systems around the world providing variety of information and communication facilities. In other word it is network of network. And the ability of the internet to Receive and transmit information from one place to another through different servers and system is called internet technology. Internet technology has been important topic in this new era as it allows users to communicate to each other faster and easily with means of computing devices. So how does internet works..? That’s a good question, when we try to share any data or information it travels in the form of packets from one router to another router until it reaches to main server from where the data is then delivered to its main destination. The movement of packets from one router to another router is called as hop.

Impact of Internet Technology to our daily lives

Internet is a technology of freedom. Over the years as technology has been improving, it has made our daily lives easier, better, faster and more fun. With growing of new devices like tablets, smartphones, smart watches and voice assistance devices (Siri, Alexa, Bixby) we are able to do things like buy clothes, buy and sell furniture’s, food delivery, reading online books, listening to music, booking, play online games and many more just by sitting inside the home. Technology has not only changed people but also the companies, institutions and organizations. Back then, the main form of communication was to use physical letters taking weeks or months to have them sent to someone and expecting a response, even longer. While now with technologies we are able to send any messages through emails or Facebook messages instantly. Similarly on shopping back then people had to walk like 30-40 minutes to buy their favorite clothes consuming a lot of time for a single clothes whereas now with a single click on a shopping websites, people can buy more than one clothes without any effort. This really proves how the internet technologies has changed on how we used to live our lives before.

It’s true that internet technologies has changed people’s lives but on what ways..? Is internet all for good? It’s a question which really is quite hard to explain. It has been found that spending hours on hours spending in internet has leads to serious health problem. People suffering from heart disease, diabetes, high blood pressure are also the one who stay on devices for many hours. Internet is effective when we use it properly but at the same time it’s dangerous if using it for hours.
